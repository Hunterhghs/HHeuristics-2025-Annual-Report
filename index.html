1. Company Overview

1.1 Mission & Positioning

HHeuristics is a research and insights firm that helps businesses make sharper, faster, and more defensible decisions in complex markets. Through consulting engagements and published research, we transform fragmented information into clear narratives and actionable recommendations for senior leaders informing strategy, product development, and capital allocation decisions.
